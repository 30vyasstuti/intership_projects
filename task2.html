Biography

A.P.J.Abdul Kalam was born on 15 october 1931, at Rameshwaram in Tamil Nadu, Dr Avul Pakir Jainulabseen Abdul Kalam specialized in aeronatical engineering from Madras Institue of Technology. Dr kalam is one of the distinguished scientists of India and has received honorary doctorates from forty-five universities and institutions in India and abroad

Early Life

Avul Pkir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born on 15 october 1931 to a Tamil Muslim Family in a pilgrimage centre of Rameshwaram on Pamban Island,then in the Madras Presidency and now in the States of Tamil Nadu.His father Jainulabdeen was a boat owner and imam of mosque;his mother Ashiamma was a housewife.Kalam was a youngest of brothers and sister of the family.

Death and Legacy

on 27 july 2015, Kalam travelled to Shillong to deliver a lecture at Indian Institute of Management Shillong.Where he collapsed and died due to cardiac arrest.
